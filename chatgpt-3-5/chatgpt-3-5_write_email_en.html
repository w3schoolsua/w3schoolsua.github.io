Using ChatGPT-3.5 to Write Emails

You can use ChatGPT to help you write different types of emails.

Use ChatGPT as an assistant, helping you put the right tone and format for your message.

Define the Goal of Your Email

To help you write an email, ChatGPT needs context. What is the goal of your email?

Examples:

  • Introduction to a potential client
  • Asking for a meeting
  • Job application
  • Company announcement
  • Formal request
  • Formal complaint
  • Response to a complaint

We will go through each of these with examples, and by the end of this page, you should have a good idea of how to use ChatGPT to write any email you need.

Email - Introduction to Potential Client

In this example, we want to showcase our services W3Schools Spaces, to a school with a web development course.

The school is called "Nowhere High", and the web development teacher is responsible for the curriculum and purchases for this course. Her name is "Nott Bernatto".

Example

With the following prompt:

Write a professional email introducing our services to a potential client. The product is detailed here: "https://www.w3schools.com/spaces/". The client's name is "Nott Bernatto", and she is in charge of the web development course on "Nothing High", a high school in New York.

A response from ChatGPT-3.5 could be:

Subject: Introducing Cutting-Edge Web Development Services for "Nothing High"

Dear Ms. Bernatto,

I hope this email finds you well. My name is [Your Name], and I am reaching out to introduce myself and the web development services offered by our company. Having recently come across your esteemed institution’s web development course at "Nothing High", I am confident that our expertise and dedication to empovering students in their journey to becoming proficient web developers align perfectly with your goals.

At [Your Company], we specialize in providing comprehensive and innovative web development solutions tailored to meet the evolving needs of educational institutions. We understand the importance of equipping students with practical skills and knowledge to excel in the ever-growing digital landscape. After thoroughly reviewing the web development course details on your website, we believe that our services can greatly complement your curriculum.

Allow me to highlight the key features and benefits of our services:

  1. Comprehensive Web Development Platform: Our web development platform offers an intuitive and user-friendly interface, designed to simplify the learning process for students at all skill levels. The platform provides hands-on coding experience, real-time preview functionality, and a wide range of educational resources.
  2. Extensive Curriculum Support: Our team of experienced web developers and educators has meticulously developed a comprehensive curriculum aligned with industry standards. The curriculum covers a diverse range of topics, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, responsive design, and more. Our resources include tutorials, exercises, quizzes, and projects, enabling students to reinforce their knowledge and skills effectively.
  3. Real-World Project Integration: We strongly believe in the value of practical learning. Our platform enables students to apply their theoretical knowledge by engaging in real-world project. These projects simulate professional web development scenarios and foster creativity and problem-solving abilities.
  4. Progress Tracking and Assessment: Our platform offers robust progress tracking and assessment features. Teachers can monitor students’ performance, provide personalized feedback, and track their progress throughout the course. This empowers educators with valuable insights into individual student strengths and areas for improvement.

To learn more about our services and explorer our web development platform in details, I invite you to visit our website at [URL: https://www.w3schools.com/spaces/]. Here, you will find comprehensive information about our platform’s features, testimonials from satisfied educational institutions, and contact details to schedule a personalized demonstration tailored to your requirements.

We are dedicated to partnering with educational institutions like "Nothing High" and fostering a supportive and enriching learning environment for students. We firmly believe that our services can contribute significantly to your web development course, equipping students with the practical skills and knowledge they need to excel in the field.

I would be delighted to discuss further how our services align with your vision and explorer potential collaboration opportunities. Please feel free to reach out to me directly at [Your Email Address] or [Your Phone Number]. I am available at your convenience to address any questions or concerns you may have.

Thank you for considering our web development services for "Nothing High". We look forward to the possibility of working together to empower the next generation of web developers.

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Position/Title]
[Your Company]
[Your Contact Information]

Now, you can continue to refine this draft and make it your own. But ChatGPT gave us a good starting point.

Email - Asking for a Meeting

In this example, we have made initial contact with "Nott Bernatto" from the example above, and we are trying to set up a meeting via Google Hangouts.

We are typically available for an hour at 16:00 GMT on Mondays and Thursdays but are flexible if that does not work for the client.

We want to discuss the possible plans available and answer any technical questions they might have.

Example

With the following prompt:

Write a professional email requesting a meeting for a discussion about using the product "W3Schools Spaces" (detailed here "https://www.w3schools.com/spaces/" ). The client has expressed interest and wants to know more about the product. The subject of the meeting is going through technical details and available plans. The client's name is "Nott Bernatto", and she is in charge of the web development course at "Nothing High", a high school in New York. We are typically available for an hour at 16:00 GMT on Mondays and Thursdays via Zoom or Google Hangouts but are flexible if that does not work for the client.

A response from ChatGPT-3.5 could be:

Subject: Request for Meeting: Exploring W3Schools Spaces for Web development Course at Nothing High.

Dear Ms. Nott Bernatto,

I hope this email finds you well. I am writing to follow up on our recent conversation, where you expressed your interest in learning more about W3Schools Spaces for the web development course at Nothing High. I would like to schedule a meeting to discuss the technical details and available plans of this product in greater depth.

W3Schools Spaces is an exceptional web development platform, detailed on the official website at "https://w3schools.com/spaces/". It offers a comprehensive suite of tools, resources, and tutorials specifically designed for educational institutions, empowering students with an immersive and collaborative learning experience.

To better understand your requirements and onjectives, I kindly request a meeting at your convenience. During this meeting, we can delve into the technical aspects of W3Schools Spaces and explorer how it can be seamlessly integrated into the web development course curriculum at Nothing High.

Proposed Meeting Details:
Date: [Specify date(s) that work best for you].
Time: 16:00 GMT (1-hour duration), or please suggest an alternative time.
Mode of Communication: Zoom or Google Hangouts (please specify your preference).

Meeting Agenda:

  1. Introduction to W3Schools Spaces: A detailed overview of the platform’s features, functionalities, and benefits for educational institutions.
  2. Technical integration: Exploring the seamless integration of W3Schools Spaces into the existing web development course framework at Nothing High.
  3. Hands-on Demo: Demonstrating the practical application of W3Schools Spaces to facilitate interactive learning and collaboration among students.
  4. Available Plans and Pricing: Reviewing the different subscription plans and pricing options tailored to educational institutions, ensuring the best fit for your specific needs.

If you have any specific questions or additional topics you would like to address during our meeting, please feel free to let me know in advance. I will ensure that our discussion covers all relevant areas of interest.

While our typical availability for a meeting is at 16:00 GMT on Mondays and Thursdays, I understand the importance of accommodating your schedule. If these times are not suitable for you, kindly propose an alternative date and time, and we will make the necessary adjustments accordingly.

To facilitate our meeting, please let me know your preferred mode of communication between Zoom and Google Hangouts. We will ensure that the necessary arrangements are made to connect with you seamlessly.

Thank you for considering this meeting request. I am confident that W3Schools Spaces will significantly enhance the web development learning experience for you students at Nothing High. I look forward to the opportunity to present it to you in details and discuss how we can collaborate to support the growth of your web development course.

If you have any questions or require further information before our meeting, please do not hesitate to reach out to me. I can be contacted at [your contact information].

Thank you for your attention, and I eagerly await your response.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
[Your Title/Position]
[Your Company]
[Contact Information]

Email - Job Application

In this example, we want to apply for a job at W3Schools.com.

We will attach our CV to this email.

We saw a LinkedIn post asking for developers. We have had 2 years of experience as a junior programmer and are very familiar with W3Schools.com.

Example

With the following prompt:

Write a professional email responding to a LinkedIn post asking for developers at W3Schools.com. I have 2 years of working experience as a junior developer and am very familiar with the company. My CV will be attached to this email.

A response from ChatGPT-3.5 could be:

Subject: Application for Developer Position at W3Schools.com.

Dear [Hiring Manager’s Name],

I hope this email finds you well. I recently came across your LinkedIn post regarding the developer positions available at W3Schools.com, and I am writing to express my keen interest in joining your esteemed organization. As a junior developer with 2 years of working experience, I am confident that my skills and familiarity with W3Schools.com make me an ideal candidate for the role.

I have been an avid user and learner on W3Schools.com for quite some time now, and I am impressed by the platform’s comprehensive resources, tutorials, and interactive learning environment. It has played a significant role in shaping my skills as a developer and enhancing my understanding of web development concepts. I am thrilled at the opportunity to contribute to the growth and success of W3Schools.com as a member of your development team.

I have attached my curriculum vitae (CV) to this email, witch provides a detailed overview of my educational background, work experience, and technical skills. During my time as a junior developer, I have gained practical experience in web development, including proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various front-end frameworks. I have also worked with back-end technologies such as PHP and MySQL, along with experience in version control systems like Git. Additionally, I have a strong understanding of responsive design principles and an eye for detail, ensuring high-quality and user-friendly web applications.

I am particularly drawn to the collaborative and innovative work culture at W3Schools.com, where creativity and continuous learning are encouraged. The prospect of contributing to the development of cutting-edge tools and resources that empower learners and developers around the world is truly exciting to me.

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss my qualifications further and how I can contribute to the growth and success of W3Schools.com. Please feel free to reach out to me to schedule an interview at your convenience. I am available for a video call or an in-person interview, depending on your preference.

Thank you for considering my application. I am excited about the possibility of joining the W3Schools.com team and leveraging my skills to further enhance the platform’s offerings. I look forward to the opportunity to contribute to the success of W3Schools.com as a developer.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
[Your Contact Information].
