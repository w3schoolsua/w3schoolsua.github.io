Australian Color Standard

Before 1985, Australians used the British color standard BS 381C.

In 1985 the Australian Independent Colour Standard AS 2700 was created:

CodeColor
BBlue
GGreen
NNeutrals (grey)
PPurple
RRed
TBlue/Green
XYellow/Red
YYellow

The codes (or names) cannot be used in HTML.
